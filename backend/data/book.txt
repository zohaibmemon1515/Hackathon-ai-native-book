# In a real scenario, this would be the full text of the AI Robotics book.

The rise of artificial intelligence and robotics is transforming our world.
This book provides a comprehensive introduction to the exciting field of AI-powered humanoid robotics.
We will explore the key concepts, technologies, and challenges in building and programming intelligent robots that can interact with the physical world.
From the basics of ROS 2 to advanced topics like digital twin simulation and VLA integration, this book will guide you on a journey to create your own AI-powered robot.
The capstone project will challenge you to build a humanoid robot that can perform complex tasks using visual language grounding.
Join us on this adventure into the future of robotics.
